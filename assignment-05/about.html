J.K. Rowling

J.K. Rowling is a British author best known for her series Harry Potter. Her real name is Joanne, but her publisher requested she use a pen name because he felt young boys would not read a book written by a female. She was born on July 31st, 1965 in Yate, England. Growing up she surrounded herself with books. At the age of six she wrote her first book. At eleven she wrote her first novel. She is now a world reknowned author.
